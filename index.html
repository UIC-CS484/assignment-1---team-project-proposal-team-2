Store Anywhere

Rest api will be used?

No external restful apis will be used as we will create our own.

User's auth collected data will be as follows:

  • username/password
  • email (to verify the registration)

The most valuable tables/fields in the db would be as follows:

  • Users -> id, username, password, email
  • Tokens -> all fields for the auth based actions
  • User's Data Tables -> user_id, corresponding_table_id

People to target:

  • middle business owners/product managers
  • starter business owners/product managers
  • independent individuals who might want to sell their products in any form

Site Goals

  • Provide elegant and easy-to-use way to upload/update/get user's products/items/goods
  • Implement easy-to-understand UX and UI
  • Create an additional API layer for the other programmers to give them the opportunity to pull/update the specific user's data

Why?

Because it will give opportunity to those people who want to extend their business further...
